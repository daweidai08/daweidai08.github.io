Plant Epigenetics, Aging & Genome Regulation

The Dai Lab studies how epigenetic information shapes plant development, aging, regeneration, and crop traits. We combine genomics, methylome profiling, chromatin biology, genetics, and computational approaches in Arabidopsis and maize.

Explore Research Join the Lab

Research focus

Epigenetic aging · DNA methylation · Chromatin regulation · Plant regeneration · Maize leaf senescence · Imprinting · Multi-omics

Research

We aim to understand how epigenetic programs are established, maintained, remodeled, and lost across plant life histories.

Epigenetic Aging

We investigate how DNA methylation and chromatin states change during plant organ aging, and how these changes relate to developmental timing and cellular identity.

Maize Senescence & Yield

We study how leaf aging, source-sink interactions, and epigenetic regulation influence grain filling, stay-green traits, and crop productivity.

Regeneration & Reprogramming

We explore epigenetic barriers that shape plant tissue culture, transformation efficiency, and regeneration competence across genotypes and tissues.
